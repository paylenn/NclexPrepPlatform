About the Simulation

What to expect during your simulation experience

Simulation Features

  • Realistic exam interface mimicking the actual NCLEX
  • Adaptive testing technology that adjusts question difficulty
  • Timer with break functionality similar to the real exam
  • Calculator, highlighting, and strikethrough tools
  • Pop-up lab values and medication references
  • Detailed performance analysis after completion

Simulation Tips

  • Find a quiet environment free from distractions
  • Use the same strategies you plan to use on exam day
  • Take the full simulation without interruptions
  • Review your performance report thoroughly
  • Focus on content areas where you scored lowest
  • Practice with all question types, especially those that challenge you

Prepare for Your Simulation

You are about to begin: NCLEX Simulation

Time Limit: 5 hours

Questions: 75 questions

Before you begin:

  • Find a quiet space without distractions
  • Have scratch paper and a pen ready if needed
  • Plan to complete the entire simulation in one sitting
  • Use the tools provided (calculator, highlighting, etc.)
